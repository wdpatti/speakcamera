How to Install

  1. Tap the "Install Shortcut" button above
  2. Review the shortcut actions in the Shortcuts app
  3. Tap "Add Shortcut" to install
  4. Run the shortcut from your Shortcuts app, home screen, or Action Button on newer iPhones

Visual Guide: Add to Home Screen

Follow these steps to add shortcut widgets to your home screen for quick access:

1

Long press on your home screen until apps jiggle

2

3

4

Scroll down until you find Shortcuts

5

Click the icon in the center of the display twice to add two buttons then click the X, then click the icon in the widgets bar on the left

6

Select which shortcut to run (SpeakCamera)

7

Repeat the process to add the second shortcut widget on the right, StopSpeech
